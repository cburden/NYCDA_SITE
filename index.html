If your dream is a career...A life...acting in Film & Television, NYCDA is the one school that can help get you there.

Unlike traditional undergraduate theater programs, at the New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts we give you today's most relevant, real-world education as you earn your acting degree…

You're on camera every day. Performing on set. Creating webisodes.Learning from industry pros. And in your 2nd year, you can start going on real auditions — for TV and film roles, commercials and digital media — building your professional reel and network of casting directors, producers, directors, fellow actors and mentors.

So when graduates from other programs arrive in New York City, you’re already years ahead of them — in who you know, in auditioning and getting cast, and in becoming a working actor in the film and television industry.

If this is the life you were born to live, we invite you to contact admissions today and set up your audition.
