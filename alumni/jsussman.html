Josh Sussman’s career took off after his 2004 graduation from The New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts.  He performed in scads of National Commercials and Television shows on NBC, Nickelodeon, The Disney Channel, and most recently on the hit FOX TV series “GLEE”.  Josh’s ability is not limited to the screen, he started out on stage and was the 2005 Nominee for Best Lead Actor in Brighton Beach Memoirs at the Perry Awards.  Although Josh is always busy in Los Angeles, he takes time to come back to NYCDA to visit the faculty who nurtured his talent and ability.
