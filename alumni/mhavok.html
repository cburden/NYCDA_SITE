• More Success Stories

Tucson native, Mike Havok, came to New York City to attend the New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts to pursue his dreams of becoming an actor. While he was still studying here at NYCDA, Mike held internships at Stark Naked Productions and Joan Lynn Casting, allowing him to witness firsthand the casting process in the business of acting. Soon after graduation from NYCDA, Mike was noticed for his performance as "Clifford Anderson" in the Fort Hill Players production of the intense thriller Deathtrap. Mike is considered by many to be a natural actor, with an easygoing control, and a unique dry sense of humor that “separates him from the pack”.

Hard-working Mike keeps auditioning in NYC for on-camera commercials, industrials, and voice-overs for television and radio. Bookings include McDonald's, Cingular, Wal-Mart, MTVu, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Sbarro, and the Travel Channel.
