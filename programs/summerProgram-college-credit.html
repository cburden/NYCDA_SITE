College Credit

Credit for the Summer Acting Training Program may be available upon successful completion of the program. The New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts is an institutionally accredited member of the National Association of Schools of Theatre and is chartered by the New York State Board of Regents. Students should inquire with a potential receiving institution regarding credit transferrability.
