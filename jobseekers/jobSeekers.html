At NYCDA, you’ll find an environment where faculty and staff are invested in the success of every student.  If you’re interested in helping to make the dreams of young actors come true, consider joining us.


To apply for a position, or to inquire about openings, please e-mail your resume and salary requirements to jobs@sft.edu.

Qualified candidates of diverse ethnic and racial backgrounds are encouraged to apply for vacant positions at all levels.
