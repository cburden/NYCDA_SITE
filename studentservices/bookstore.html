The New York Conservatory for Dramatic Arts bookstore is at Barnes and Noble on Fifth Avenue and 18th Street in New York. All of the required books (check here for titles) can be purchased there. Books also can be ordered online thru Barnes and Noble or other online retailers such as Amazon by referencing the ISBN number of the book. ISBN numbers are provided in the book lists.
